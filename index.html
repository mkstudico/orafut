Frequently Asked Questions

What is ORAFUT exactly?

ORAFUT is a hybrid platform: part football prediction tracker, part social network. Unlike betting sites where you play against the house, ORAFUT connects you with experts ("Predictors") to help you make better decisions.

Is the Free Trial genuinely free?

Yes. You get 3 days of full "User" access. You do not need to enter any payment information to start the trial. It expires automatically.

Can I become a Predictor later?

Yes. If you start as a User and consistently make correct votes (earning points), our system may invite you to upgrade. Alternatively, you can sign up directly as a Predictor if you are confident.

How do payments work?

We use Paypack Rwanda for secure Mobile Money transactions (MTN/Airtel). Payments are processed instantly and securely.
