Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ORAFUT

What is ORAFUT exactly?

ORAFUT is a football-focused social platform that combines community voting, expert predictions, and real-time discussions. It's where football fans connect, share insights, and build their football reputation together.

Who is a predictor on ORAFUT?

A predictor is a football analyst or enthusiast who shares match predictions and analysis with the community. Predictors build followers, earn recognition through accurate predictions, and contribute to the platform's football intelligence.

Who is a user on ORAFUT?

A user is any football fan who joins ORAFUT to vote on matches, follow predictors, participate in discussions, and enjoy football content. Users earn points for correct votes and build their football profile.

How do votes work on ORAFUT?

Users can vote on upcoming matches by selecting their predicted outcome. The community's collective votes are displayed as percentages. Users earn points for correct votes, which contribute to their profile score.

Can predictors earn from ORAFUT?

Predictors on ORAFUT focus on building their reputation and followers through accurate predictions and valuable analysis. Based on performance and community impact, predictors may unlock various opportunities on the platform.

Is ORAFUT mobile-first?

Yes! ORAFUT is designed with mobile users in mind. The platform works beautifully on smartphones, tablets, and desktops, ensuring football fans can participate anytime, anywhere.
